Frequently Asked Questions

What is an eVisa?

An eVisa is an electronic visa that allows you to apply for a visa online without visiting an embassy.

How long does processing take?

Processing usually takes 3-5 business days after submission of complete application.

What documents do I need?

You need a valid passport, recent photo, travel itinerary, and proof of accommodation.
